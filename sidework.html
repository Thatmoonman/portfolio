MUSIC

Music has always been a huge part of my life. I grew up listening to my Dad play guitar in the basement. When I was in my teens, I picked up his old acoustic and never really put it down again. I spent many years honing my craft (ie writing a lot of pretty bad songs and maybe a few good ones alongs the way), and after graduating college I decided to head to New York City and see what I could make of it. I've played in a couple bands and put out a few records. Life and the pandemic made making music very difficult and I have since turned my creative attention to other pursuits.
